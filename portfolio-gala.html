Golden Anniversary Gala

Ballroom Celebration 200 Guests August 2026

The Vision

A lavish 50th anniversary celebration in a grand ballroom. The couple wanted an opulent gold theme that felt timeless and celebratory, with every detail reflecting five decades of love.

What We Designed

  • Venue Styling — Floor-to-ceiling gold draping at the entrance, with a custom monogram gobo projected onto the dance floor.
  • Table Design — Gold charger plates, crystal candelabras with taper candles, gold flatware, and champagne-colored linens. Centerpieces featured gold-sprayed branches with hanging crystals.
  • Seating — Gold banquet chairs with ivory cushions for 25 round tables, plus a raised head table with a draped backdrop and floral garland.
  • Lighting — Warm amber uplighting around the ballroom perimeter, crystal chandeliers, and pin-spot lighting on each centerpiece.
  • Cake — A five-tier fondant cake with gold leaf detailing, sugar flowers, and a custom gold cake topper of the anniversary year.
  • Dessert Bar — Gold-dusted macarons, mini champagne cupcakes, chocolate truffles, and a tiered display with fresh flowers.
  • Photo Area — A gold sequin backdrop with a vintage frame prop station and instant photo printing.

Services Provided

Full Event Design Decoration & Styling Furniture & Seating Lighting Cake & Desserts
