Baby Shower Brunch

Intimate Brunch 40 Guests September 2026

The Vision

A sweet, playful baby shower with a soft pink palette. The mom-to-be wanted a modern, Instagram-worthy celebration that felt warm and personal — not overly formal.

What We Designed

  • Balloon Installation — An organic balloon garland in blush, rose, gold, and white spanning 4 meters across the main wall, with cascading ribbon tails.
  • Dessert Table — A styled sweets display featuring custom sugar cookies shaped as baby items, pink macarons, cake pops, and a two-tier buttercream cake with fondant flowers.
  • Table Settings — Round tables with blush linen runners, gold geometric place card holders, mini succulent favors, and pink floral bud vases.
  • Seating — Mix of white folding chairs and blush velvet accent chairs for the gift-opening area, with a cushioned lounge corner.
  • Signage — Custom acrylic welcome sign, a chalkboard menu for the brunch spread, and a "Wishes for Baby" card station with gold pens.
  • Photo Wall — A flower wall backdrop in pink and ivory with a gold frame prop and letter board for custom messages.

Services Provided

Decoration & Styling Cake & Desserts Furniture & Seating Floral Design Signage
