Rose Garden Wedding

Outdoor Ceremony 150 Guests June 2026

The Vision

The couple dreamed of a romantic outdoor celebration surrounded by nature. They wanted a soft, timeless palette of blush pinks, ivory, and gold accents that would complement the garden's natural beauty.

What We Designed

  • Ceremony Arch — A 3-meter floral arch with cascading roses, peonies, and eucalyptus in blush and ivory tones, framed with sheer fabric draping.
  • Table Settings — Gold-rimmed charger plates, crystal glassware, and linen napkins tied with fresh rosemary sprigs. Each table featured a low centerpiece of garden roses and candles.
  • Seating — Chiavari gold chairs for the ceremony and cross-back wooden chairs for the reception, creating a warm, natural feel.
  • Lighting — Bistro string lights overhead with scattered pillar candles and lanterns lining the garden pathways.
  • Cake — A four-tier buttercream cake with hand-painted gold leaf details and fresh floral accents matching the ceremony arch.
  • Signage — Acrylic welcome sign with gold calligraphy, matching table numbers, and a hand-lettered seating chart on a vintage mirror.
